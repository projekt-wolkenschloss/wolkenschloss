# Our Philosophy

We are motivated by two trends, that are underlying to our vision and mission:

1. The growing amassment of data of individuals by public and paid services and the resulting privacy concerns.
2. The web5 ideology, that everybody will own some personal compute sometime that acts totally decentralized on their behalf.

Furthermore, we believe that,

- knowledge should be free and open source
- information and technology should be free and accessible to all
- sustainability is not an option, it is a must
- keeping it simple is important
- technology should serve humans, not the other way around
- everybody has the right to repair their own stuff
- surveillance capitalism is a threat to our society and democracy
- corporate control over personal data is detrimental to individual freedom and autonomy
- self-hosting is a powerful way to reclaim control over one's digital life
- self-hosting should be accessible to everyone, not just tech enthusiasts
